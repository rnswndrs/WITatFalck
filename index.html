🎀 Who We Are

We're a community of women across Falck who work in technology — developers, analysts, project managers, designers, and everyone in between. We come together to support each other, share knowledge, and make tech at Falck a place where women thrive.

🎀 Our Purpose

To create a welcoming space where women in tech can connect, grow, and inspire one another. We believe that diverse perspectives build better technology, and we're here to champion visibility, mentorship, and belonging within Falck's tech community.

🎀 Our Journey

December 2025
The idea takes shape — a few passionate colleagues start the conversation about creating a community for women in tech at Falck.
Early 2026
Women in Tech at Falck is officially launched. Our Teams group chat goes live and the first members join.
2026 — Present
Growing our network, hosting conversations, sharing opportunities, and building a stronger, more connected community every day.
The Future
Events, mentorship, knowledge-sharing sessions, and a louder voice for women in tech across all of Falck.
